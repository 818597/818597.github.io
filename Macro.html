Macro Play

What is Macro Play?

Macro play is pushing your advantages outside of your lane and onto the rest of the map. Teleporting bot lane to assist your botlaners in a fight is a good example of Macro Play. The thought process should be similar to this surrounding Macro Play: "(Action) was done/is going to be done, which will (effect), allowing us/them or preventing us/them to/from (reaction). "
